Places I MayNeverGo: The Galapagos Islands

Welcome to the Galapagos Islands!

The Galapagos Islands are a group of islands the Pacific Ocean. They were formed by volcanoes that rose up from the bottom of the ocean. Because they are far from other places like South America, there are many plants and animals here that live no where else on Earth! Come along on an adventure and learn more about the Galapagos!
